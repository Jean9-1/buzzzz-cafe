About Us

Buzzzz Café was created to draw more visitors to our picturesque village of Vavatsinia, a haven for outdoor enthusiasts. Nestled at the village entrance, our café is the perfect stop for hikers and mountain bikers looking to explore the stunning trails and rich history of the area. While the village is home to several traditional tavernas, we saw an opportunity to offer something different. With a spacious terrace that boasts breathtaking mountain views, Buzzzz Café serves up a delicious selection of breakfast options, sweet and savoury crepes, pancakes and waffles. Whether you're in the mood for a satisfying meal or just want to relax with a coffee or cold drink, we've got you covered. Our family-friendly café also offers creative workshops, including cheese making, candle making, seasonal decoration crafting, T-shirt painting, and much more. At Buzzzz Café, there's always something to enjoy—whether you're here for the food, the views, or a fun hands-on experience!
